LevelUp Scanner


The standard LevelUp scanner is the Honeywell 7580 2D barcode reader. It is compatible with USB and Serial and can be connected straight to the workstation or via bluetooth dongle to iPads.

Documentation and additional drivers (including OPOS) can be downloaded here.
